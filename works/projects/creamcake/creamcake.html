Light Design | Creamcake 3hd 2025: Vertigo
Berlin, 2025

Working with light designer Shaly López, developed spatial previsualization and simulation using Capture Lighting and Blender, alongside live lighting operation with GrandMA3 and live laser programming in TouchDesigner throughout 3hd 2025: Vertigo Festival for Creamcake in Berlin. Worked with performing artists including TOURS, Mug (Ludwig Wandinger & Yves B Golden), Joshua Woolford w/ Sippin’ T & Lie Ning, Star & Stan, and Kinlaw, developing site-specific lighting compositions and immersive stage environments tailored to each artist’s performance and sonic identity.
