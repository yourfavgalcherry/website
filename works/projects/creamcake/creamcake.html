Light Design | Creamcake 3hd 2025: Vertigo
Berlin, 2025

GrandMA3 lighting programming, and live laser and lighting operation throughout the 3hd 2025: Vertigo festival in Berlin. Worked with performing artists including TOURS, Mug (Ludwig Wandinger & Yves B Golden), Star & Stan, and Kinlaw developing site-specific lighting compositions and immersive stage environments aligned with Creamcake’s curatorial vision.
