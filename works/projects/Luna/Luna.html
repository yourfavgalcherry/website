Light Design | Luna x Unguarded
Berlin, 2025 | Event Link

Designed and implemented a custom DMX x Laser light system using TouchDesigner for this Berlin-based club night hosted by avant-label Unguarded and collective LUNA. Integrated laser and strobe lighting through LED pixel mapping. Lighting simulations and stage visuals were developed and pre-rendered using Capture Visualization. Executed the final real-time setup and live-reactive control, creating a euphoric, high-intensity audiovisual experience that complemented performances by Xenia Reaper, Amore Marco, Yui, Seychelles, Alex Wang, Cybermission, and TRYCE.
