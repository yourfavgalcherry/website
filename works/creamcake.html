Luminal
VR Installation · Berlin · 2024

Luminal is a virtual reality work exploring perception, presence, and light as a spatial and emotional medium. The project constructs an immersive environment where light behaves as both architecture and narrative force.

Developed using real-time engines and spatial audio, the work responds dynamically to viewer movement, creating a constantly shifting perceptual field. Rather than offering a fixed narrative, Luminal invites the audience into a speculative space shaped by rhythm, intensity, and sensory resonance.

The project premiered at Manifest:IO Symposium in Berlin and was later presented as a narrative adaptation in TOO SOON TO SAY at Bayerischer Hof, Munich.
