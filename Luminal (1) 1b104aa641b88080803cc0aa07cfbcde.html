Introduction

  • A journey into the deep ocean to explore solitude, digital estrangement, and fragmented identity through the lens of a fictional bioluminescent creature

Research Question

  • How can bioluminescence, traditionally an organic phenomenon, be reimagined as a speculative digital language for storytelling, emotion, and connection in virtual worlds?
  • What does the interplay between light and darkness in this work reveal about human attempts to create connection and meaning in spaces of isolation, both physical and digital?
  • How does the simulation’s use of light as a medium transcend aesthetics to function as a tool for communication, emotional resonance, and fragmented identity in post-collapse environments?

Idea Generation

From Naturkundemuseum Wet Collection to the Bioluminescent Abyss

  • WET COLLECTION

    The project begins with inspiration from Berlin Naturkundemuseum Wet Collection, where preserved marine life symbolizes fragmentation, isolation, and suspended existence.

    This imagery inspires Luminal, a fictional bioluminescent creature navigating a post-apocalyptic world where the surface has collapsed, leaving only the abyss.

  • LIGHT

    The starting point explores light as a language, using bioluminescence to reflect themes of digital estrangement, fragmented identity, and resilience.

    Combining personal experiences of disconnection with speculative storytelling, the work uses simulation to immerse audiences in a world where decay fosters connection and transformation amidst collapse.

  • LUMINAL

    The Luminal is a fictional bioluminescent creature born from the collapse of the surface world, drawn into the abyss by an unidentified light.

    Altered by the depths, it fuses organic and digital elements, using bioluminescence to communicate and navigate its fragmented memories.

    As a spectral nomad, it searches for connection and meaning amidst the isolation of the post-apocalyptic deep sea.

Persona/Creatures Design

  • Persona: Luminal

    As Luminal, players navigate the post-apocalyptic deep sea, uncovering fragmented memories and using bioluminescence as a tool for communication and survival. The player becomes an active participant in shaping the narrative, influencing the environment and interacting with other glowing lifeforms in the abyss.

  • Creatures

    The creatures Luminal encounters are inspired by real deep-sea bioluminescent organisms but have been transformed by the Anthropocene, bearing mutations from pollutants, microplastics, and digital distortions.

    They communicate through intricate light patterns, using bioluminescence as a language to send warnings, express emotions, and share fragmented memories. Each luminescence interaction between Luminal and the creatures drives the narrative, reflecting themes of the project.

Spatial Setting

  • Destroyed Surface

    The journey begins at the surface, where the world is destroyed and unrecognizable.

    The overwhelming unidentifiable light draws The Luminal into the depths, forcing it to confront a post-apocalyptic reality.

  • The Abyssal Zone

    The next sequence begins in the Abyssal Zone, skipping intermediate depths as The Luminal is suddenly plunged into the deep ocean by the unidentified light.

    In this lightless void, the setting is hauntingly still, with faint glowing organisms breaking through the silence.

    This zone is filled with eerie shapes and strange lifeforms, hinting at the beginning of The Luminal's exploration.

  • The Midnight Zone

    The Luminal continues its descent into the Midnight Zone, encountering glowing beings that emit bioluminescent signals.

    This area is alive with faint, flickering lights, as The Luminal begins to decipher patterns in the glow.

  • The Sunlight Zone

    The journey concludes in the Sunlight Zone, which is reimagined as a glowing ecosystem of bioluminescent harmony.

Sound Design

The sound design for Luminal blends field recordings of underwater currents, and sediment shifts with digitally synthesized textures to evoke the alien, bioluminescent world of the deep ocean. Each creature The Luminal encounters is given a unique "voice" through rhythmic, tonal patterns, turning their light signals into an auditory language.

For the sound design, They used Ambisonic audio to create a spatial mix, enhancing immersion in the VR environment.

  • The sound dynamically shifts based on the creature’s movement, allowing players to perceive depth and direction within the 360 space.
  • This technique ensures that sound sources feel natural and correctly positioned, reinforcing the sense of presence in VR.

Fascinated by the fusion of sound and technology, David crafts DIY installations and visual performances, like his 2024 collaborations with Samo Benko at Kuumšt and RealShit Festival, using iPhone cameras, MacBooks, and projectors to create immersive experiences.

His 2023 debut EP, INFORMICA, features DIY recordings of household electronics, merging breakcore, vapor twitch, and drum and bass. He now focuses on game sound design and Arduino-based interactive art, reflecting his passion for innovation.
