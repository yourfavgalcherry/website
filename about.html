Chaery Yoon is a Seoul-born, Berlin-based visual artist and creative technologist specializing in interactive, light-based visuals and real-time audiovisual environments. With a background in sculpture, she expands her practice into the digital realm crafting immersive, reactive spaces using tools such as TouchDesigner, Unreal Engine, AI, sensors and Lights. Her work often explores speculative narratives and humanities-driven phenomena, creating emotionally resonant experiences through worldbuilding and sensory storytelling.

Chaery’s visual language blends atmospheric abstraction with precise real-time control, often responding directly to sound, performance, and audience movement. Her visuals are heavily light-driven, playing with intensity, rhythm, and texture to generate a heightened sense of presence and emotion in both physical and digital environments.
