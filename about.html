Chaery Yoon

Bio

Chaery Yoon(She/Her) is a visual artist and creative technologist specializing in interactive, real-time visuals and audio-visual experiences. With a background in sculpture, she extends her practice into the digital realm, using tools such as AI, TouchDesigner, Unreal Engine, and sensors to craft immersive environments that respond to human interaction.

Synthesizing technology, humanities-based phenomena, and speculative narratives through worldbuilding methods, she focuses on creating sensory and emotional experiences that engage audiences.
